Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2024

Introduction

Windows CPU & GPU Temperature Monitor ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

Our application collects the following information:

  • CPU temperature data
  • GPU temperature data
  • System information necessary for temperature monitoring

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the purpose of displaying temperature readings in the application. We do not:

  • Store any collected data
  • Share data with third parties
  • Use data for any purpose other than temperature monitoring

Data Security

All temperature monitoring is performed locally on your device. We do not transmit any data over the internet or store it on external servers.

Third-Party Services

Our application uses OpenHardwareMonitorLib for temperature monitoring. This library operates entirely on your local machine and does not transmit any data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
